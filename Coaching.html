UF Coaching Institute Certification

Completing the University of Florida Coaching Institute equipped me with the skills to effectively coach colleagues and peers. Through this certification, I developed expertise in identifying areas for growth, collecting and analyzing instructional data, and fostering meaningful reflective conversations. These skills position me as an ideal candidate for roles requiring collaboration, professional development, and data-driven decision-making.

Key Components of the Coaching Process:

  • Indicator Selection: Collaboratively identified an instructional or work-related indicator for improvement.
  • Process Design: Outlined a structured plan including a 15-minute observation to track data on the selected indicator.
  • Data Collection: Conducted focused observations and collected actionable data to inform discussions.
  • Data Analysis and Reflection: Engaged in reflective conversations with the mentee to analyze the data and determine next steps for improvement.
  • Follow-Up: Facilitated a follow-up reflection to evaluate progress and refine the approach.

Skills in Action:

This coaching process demonstrates my ability to guide others in achieving measurable improvement while fostering a supportive and professional relationship. These skills are transferable to any role that requires:

  • Strong interpersonal communication and active listening.
  • Data-driven analysis for performance improvement.
  • Facilitation of growth and development within teams or individuals.
  • Collaborative problem-solving and decision-making.
