About Me

I’m Valerie Kosters, a dedicated professional with a background in education and a passion for facilitating growth through effective training solutions. Over the past six years, I’ve honed my skills in designing and delivering engaging, results-oriented workshops and curricula.

My goal is to transition these skills into corporate training, where I can create impactful programs that drive performance and development. I thrive on collaboration and innovation, and I’m excited to bring my expertise to dynamic, learner-focused environments.
