Instructor-Led Tutorials

Google Classroom Navigation Tutorial

This instructor-led video tutorial provides a comprehensive guide on how to navigate and use Google Classroom effectively. Designed for students, parents, and educators, the video breaks down the key features of this powerful Learning Management System (LMS) in a clear and engaging way.

  • Step-by-Step Guidance: Learn how to log in, join a class, and access course materials with ease.
  • Interactive Navigation Tips: Discover how to use the Stream, Classwork, and People tabs to stay organized and connected.
  • Assignment Management: Get a detailed walkthrough of how to view, complete, and submit assignments, including attaching files and checking grades.
  • Communication Features: See how to ask questions, comment on posts, and interact with teachers and classmates.
  • Parent Insights: Learn how parents can monitor student progress and stay informed.

Submitting Assignments in Google Classroom

This instructional video provides a clear and concise guide on how to upload and turn in assignments using Google Classroom, ensuring students can easily complete their coursework. Designed for beginners, the tutorial demonstrates each step with simple instructions and practical examples.

  • Navigating the Classwork Tab: Learn how to find and open assignments posted by your teacher.
  • Uploading Work: Step-by-step guidance on attaching files, adding links, and using Google Docs or Slides to complete assignments.
  • Submitting Assignments: Understand how to mark assignments as "Turned In" and ensure your work is submitted successfully.
  • Revising Submissions: Discover how to unsubmit and update assignments if changes are needed.
  • Assignment Status Overview: Learn how to check due dates, submission status, and feedback from teachers.
